Our world

We are an international organizationof volunteers who fight to defend the value of identity, regardless of language or any other characteristic.

Our mission is to provide access to every language on earth, either spoken or signed.

The time has come for us, the people of Earth to become familiar with one another, to dispel the fear and doubt from others who, by simple distance, speak differently or hold other beliefs.
